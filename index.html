About

I am an undergraduate researcher focused on computer vision for healthcare and materials science. Recent work includes retinal image enhancement for diabetic retinopathy detection, titanium alloy microstructure segmentation, and diffusion models guided by anatomical priors for MRI generation.

I enjoy building end-to-end pipelines—from data preprocessing and augmentation to model design and evaluation—and translating research prototypes into reproducible code.

Research interests

  • Medical image generation (diffusion, Mamba-guided)
  • Medical image super-resolution
  • Medical vision-language models
  • Segmentation-guided diffusion
  • Computer vision for materials informatics
